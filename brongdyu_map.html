Brongdyu Station North
龙市北站

Brongdyu Station North is a transportation, shopping, dining, historical, and cultural hub in the center of Dragon City. The tall arched ceiling tiled in red, green, and purple; towering white marble columns; the marble floors, mosaicked with the history of South Liang; and the breathtaking statues and reliefs draw many tourists, locals, and commuters alike. Brongdyu Station Nuumsreenkhō
龙市站的南山口

Brongdyu Station in Nuumsreenkhō maintains the most important interchange in the city, bridging the connection between the city center and the surrounding suburbs.
It has a mall with many shops and restaurants, as well as a few hotels used by tourists or people who missed the last train. During morning and evening rush hour, and the last train, the station is swamped.

Revolutionary Martyrs Station

The architecture of Revolutionary Martyrs Station pays homage to the many martyrs who sacrificed their lives for Shen-Long and his royal court, as well as the SLP. Nearby is the Hall of Revolutionary Martyrs, built with a combination of brutalist and traditional styles that the station is also built after.
